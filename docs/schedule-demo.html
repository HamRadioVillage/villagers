Villagers · schedule redesign · v5 · multi-day

Keep every table manned — across every day of the con

Each activity is a position staffed continuously while the village is open, covered by a rotating mix of volunteers who each claim any window they choose. Browsing and staffing happen one day at a time (a shared time axis lets you compare positions), while "where you're needed" and "your shifts" span the whole event.

Why one day at a time

  • Volunteers filter by availability first — "I'm here Fri & Sat." Day is the primary axis, so it's a switcher, not a wall of stacks.
  • A shared time axis only works within a day — lining all positions up on 10–4 is how you read "who's bare at 2pm."
  • Each day differs — setup Friday, peak Saturday, teardown Sunday have different hours and needs.

What spans all days

  • Where you're needed — the worst gaps across the event, each tagged with its day.
  • Your shifts — your personal agenda, every day at once.
  • Covered every day? — an admin drill-in: one position stacked across Fri / Sat / Sun.
